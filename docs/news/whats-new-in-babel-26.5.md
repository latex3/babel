# What's new in babel 26.5

2026-03-24

There is a series of fixes for the standard math environments and
`amsmath`, related mainly to `multlines` and the `fleqn` option, by [Udi
Fogiel](https://github.com/Udi-Fogiel). There are further fixes on the
way, to be added in future versions.

Also fixed incorrect kashida placement with sukūn in lam–alif sequences
(#406), thanks to [Alq](https://github.com/ahmedelq).
